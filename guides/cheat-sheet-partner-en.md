# Translator App Cheat Sheet (For You)

## What this is

The **Microsoft Translator** app is already set up on your phone, joined to a shared "conversation" with Amelia's mom. You speak English, she speaks Mandarin — the app translates both ways automatically.

---

## How to use it (3 steps)

### 1. Open the app

Tap the **Microsoft Translator** icon (blue/white "T" logo).

It should already be in the shared conversation — no extra setup needed.

### 2. Press and hold the big microphone button

- **Press and hold** the large mic button in the middle of the screen
- **Speak in English**
- **Release** when you're done talking

### 3. Wait for the reply

- Mom's Mandarin will appear translated into English on your screen
- Tap the **speaker icon 🔊** next to a message to have it read aloud

---

## Tips

- Speak a bit slower and in short sentences — translation accuracy is much better with short, clear phrases
- Wait for one message to finish translating before sending the next
- If something gets translated oddly, try rephrasing and speak again

---

Message Amelia — she set this up and can help troubleshoot.
